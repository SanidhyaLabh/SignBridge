About Speech to ISL Translator

The Speech to Indian Sign Language (ISL) Translator is a cutting-edge tool designed to bridge the communication gap between spoken language and Indian Sign Language. It leverages advanced speech recognition and animation technologies to provide real-time translation, making communication more accessible for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

Features

  • Real-time speech-to-text transcription.
  • Accurate ISL grammar translation.
  • Interactive sign language animations.
  • User-friendly interface with dark mode support.

How It Works

Simply click the "Start Recording" button to begin. The tool will transcribe your speech into text, convert it into ISL grammar, and display the corresponding sign language animation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create inclusive technology that empowers individuals with hearing impairments to communicate effectively and independently.
